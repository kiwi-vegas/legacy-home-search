### Mandatory Content Angles to Work In Where Relevant

- **Military/PCS angle**: Hampton Roads has one of the largest military populations in the US (NAS Oceana, NOB Norfolk, Joint Base Langley-Eustis, Fort Story). VA loans, BAH rates, 12-month turnaround cycles — these resonate heavily.
- **Flood zone / flood insurance**: Norfolk is among the fastest-sinking cities in the US. Flood zone status materially affects insurance costs and buyer decisions. Always relevant for waterfront and Norfolk/Hampton posts.
- **Virginia-specific quirks**: Attorney closing requirement, no transfer tax (unlike many states), strong homestead exemption.
