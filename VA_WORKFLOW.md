# VA Workflow Guide

## Your Job

After Barry approves a blog article, it lands in the Content Queue for you to finish and publish. Your job is:

1. Open the post from the queue
2. Add or generate a thumbnail image
3. Review and optionally edit the Facebook caption
4. Click Publish

That's it. You don't need to know API keys, platform IDs, or anything technical.

---

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Open the Content Queue

Go to: `https://legacyhometeamlpt.com/admin/va-queue?secret=YOUR_SECRET`

You'll see two sections:
- **Needs Media** — posts waiting for a thumbnail (start here)
- **Ready to Publish** — posts with thumbnails, ready for your publish click

---

### 2. Open a Post

Click any card to open the editor. You'll see:

- **Article** — the title, category, and excerpt (read-only, for context)
- **Facebook Post Caption** — editable text that will be posted to Facebook
- **Thumbnail Builder** — where you create or upload the thumbnail
- **Publish panel** — the final publish button

---

### 3. Add a Thumbnail

You have two options:

#### Option A — Generate with AI
1. Review the pre-filled prompt (already customized for this article)
2. Edit the prompt if you want to change the style, mood, or description
3. (Optional) Select a background photo from the community grid
4. Click **✨ Generate Thumbnail**
5. Wait ~20 seconds for the image to appear in the preview

#### Option B — Upload Your Own
1. Click **Upload Image**
2. Select a JPG or PNG from your computer
3. The image appears in the preview immediately

---

### 4. Review the Caption

The Facebook caption is pre-filled automatically. Read it and edit if needed.
The blog post URL is added automatically — you don't need to add it.

---

### 5. Mark as Ready

Once you're happy with the thumbnail, click **✓ Mark as Ready**.
This saves the thumbnail to the post. The button changes to "Thumbnail saved."

---

### 6. Publish

Click **🚀 Publish**.

The system will:
- Make the blog post live on the website
- Post the article to the Legacy Home Team Facebook page

You'll see a spinner while it's working. Within a minute you should see:
- ✓ Published successfully!
- A link to the Facebook post
- A link to the blog post

---

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

**"Publish Failed"** — The system couldn't post to Facebook. Click Publish again to retry. If it keeps failing, contact the admin.

**Generated image looks wrong** — Edit the prompt and click Generate again. You can generate as many times as you want before marking ready.

**Post not in queue** — It may have already been published, or it hasn't been approved yet. Check the Legacy thumbnail review page or ask the admin.

---

## Tips

- The prompt is pre-written but you can change anything — the more specific, the better the result
- YouTube-style thumbnails work best: bold text, high contrast, simple composition
- You can upload a thumbnail you made externally (Canva, Photoshop, etc.) instead of generating one
- Always read the Facebook caption before publishing — it's auto-generated but sometimes needs a light edit
